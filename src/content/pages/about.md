If you have made it here, you probably already know a little about me.

This blog is a place where I record my journey of learning and exploration. Rather than simply collecting notes, I hope to organize my understanding of ideas, explain concepts clearly, and preserve the thoughts that emerge during the process of learning.

I believe that understanding the principles behind things is more valuable than simply learning how to use them. Therefore, most articles here focus on the ideas, designs, and underlying reasoning behind computer science, technology, and mathematics.

My personal space: [cavill.site](https://cavill.site/)
where I write about literature, history, films, and my thoughts on life.
